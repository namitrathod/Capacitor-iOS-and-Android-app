Effective date: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SplitKit ("SplitKit", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and discloses information when you use the SplitKit mobile app and related services.

6) Account Deletion

You can request deletion from within the app (Account Settings) where available. When deletion is completed, your account data is removed or anonymized as required by our system design and legal obligations.
