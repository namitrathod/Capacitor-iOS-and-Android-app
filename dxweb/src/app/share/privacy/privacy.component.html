Privacy Policy

Last updated: Mar 20, 2026

SplitKit (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides an expense-splitting service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the SplitKit app.

1. Information we collect

Account information: when you create an account, we may collect your email address and the full name you provide.

Group and expense information: when you create groups and add expenses, we collect expense details you enter (for example, descriptions, amounts, and participant information).

Device information: we may process minimal technical information required to run the app and communicate with our services.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to provide the core features of the app, including creating groups, recording expenses, and showing balances and activity.

3. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate the service (for example, between your device and our backend) and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your app uses an authentication token to access your account.

5. Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, as permitted by law, and as needed to resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, and comply with legal requirements.

6. Your choices

You can update your profile information in the app. You may also contact us to request assistance related to your account.

7. Children’s privacy

SplitKit is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@splitkit.test.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the latest changes were made.
